Victron-Energy Settings

  1. VRM Portal Id

    - Open the https://vrm.victronenergy.com/login and log in

    - On the top left corner press the ☰ icon

    - Click on your home installation name with the battery icon

    - Click on the Settings

    - Go to the General tab

    - On the open widow you should see the VRM Portal ID

    - Copy it and paste to the VRM Portal Id field

  2. Maximum Price for setting the schedule

    - Write the desired price of the kwH

    - When the price will be less or equal to the price you set the robot will make a schedule to charge the batteries

  3. Email

    - Write the email that you use to sign in to your VRM Portal

  4. Password

    - Write the Password of your instance

    5. The Email and Password are used by the robot to sign in to the instance and set the schedule
